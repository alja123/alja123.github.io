About Alexey Pokrovskiy

I am a mathematics lecturer at University College London.

I work in Discrete Mathematics and Combinatorics. I completed my PhD in the London School of Economics and Political Science under the supervision of Prof. Jan van den Heuvel and Prof. Jozef Skokan.

After finishing my PhD, I did a postdocs at Freie Universität Berlin with Prof Tibor Szabó and at ETH Zurich with Prof Benny Sudakov, and worked as a lecturer at Birkbeck.

I'm interested in Latin squares, Ramsey theory, probability, graph partitioning, Hamiltonian cycles, graph limits, directed graphs, regularity lemmas, hypergraph matchings, algorithms, and positional games.
